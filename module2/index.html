Benifits of Eating Fruits

Apple

Eating apples on a regular basis has the potential to reduce insulin resistance, which should lead to lower blood sugar levels. This is because the polyphenols stimulate your pancreas to release insulin and help your cells take in sugar.

Mango

Mango is a good source of vitamin C. The benefits of eating mangoes are, a decent source of vitamin A, folate and are pretty high in fiber, which is beneficial for colon cancer prevention, heart disease and weight control.

Grapes

Grapes are a good source of potassium, a mineral that helps balance fluids in your body. Potassium can help bring down high blood pressure and lower your risk of heart disease and stroke. so eating grapes can help fill the gap.
